Jimi Hendrix

Hendrix was one of the most influental Guitarists of the 20th century. He pioneered some of the most revered guitar techniques, and changed blues and rock music forever. He was born in Seattle, Washington, but took a lot of influence from the electric blues players in the southeastern united states.

Hendrix's Legacy

Even nearly 50 years after his death, Hendrix remains one of the most well known guitarists of all time. He died at the age of 27 on september 18th of 1970.
